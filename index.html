About

Chasing Light Across Every Landscape

I'm Rishabh Patel — a photographer driven by an obsession with the world in its most unguarded moments. What began as a curiosity with a camera has evolved into a lifelong pursuit of light, movement, and the extraordinary found within the ordinary.

My work spans three worlds: the sweeping stillness of landscapes, the raw intimacy of wildlife, and the quiet complexity of portraits. Whether I'm standing at the edge of a canyon at golden hour, waiting for a bird of prey to take flight, or framing the character in someone's expression — the goal is always the same: to freeze a moment that deserves to last.

Travel fuels everything. Each destination reshapes how I see — new light, new terrain, new life. The camera is simply how I make sense of it all.
